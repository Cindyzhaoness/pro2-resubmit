About Frontend Developer

Your website is a sum of layers—structure, data, design, content, and functionality. Creating that user-facing functionality is the job of a front-end developer. Using a combination of markup languages, design, and client-side scripts and frameworks, the front-end developer creates the environment for everything that users see and touch: content, layout, and interaction.When hiring a front-end developer, it’s helpful to have a broad view of how the front end of a site functions, what a front-end developer does, their responsibilities, and what their skills can contribute to your project.
